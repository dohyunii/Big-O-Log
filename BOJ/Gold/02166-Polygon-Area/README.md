## [[BOJ-2166] 다각형의 면적 (Polygon Area)](https://www.acmicpc.net/problem/2166)

> **Topic:** Area of a Polygon, Geometry\
> **Difficulty:** Gold V

### Complexity Analysis
* **Time Complexity:** $O(N)$
* **Space Complexity:** $O(1)$

### Mistakes & Lessons
**Mistake:** Must take the absolute value of the final area value. Bitwise shift works differently with negative numbers.
